5674 KS4 Technical Awards 2024

  • Further documentation of 5674 KS4 Technical Awards 2024
Summary
Review of qualification content overlap to support performance table discounting. The aim of this exercise is to understand whether there is an overlap in the subject content covered by the below qualifications and, if so, the extent of this overlap. This is to support allocation of discount codes to qualifications in performance tables. Discounting ensures that where a pupil has taken two or more qualifications with an overlap in curriculum, the performance tables only give credit once for teaching a single course of study.
Qualifications
WJEC Level 1/2 Vocational Award in Construction and the Built Environment (Technical Award)
City & Guilds Level 2 Technical Award in Constructing and Maintaining the Built Environment
