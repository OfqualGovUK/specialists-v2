Start another application

Apply to work with Ofqual on more subjects, qualification types, qualification levels or activities.

You can apply for new areas and reapply for areas you have previously been unsuccessful in.

An additional application involves:

  • Confirm, add to, or change your employment history and education details.
  • Choose the subjects, qualification types, qualification levels and activities you would like to apply for.
  • Submit evidence of your experience in the areas you have chosen to apply for.

You will need:

  • Details of any changes or additions to your employment history.
  • Details and certificates for any additional qualifications or other education included in your application.
  • Any further supporting evidence to clearly demonstrate your expertise in the areas you are applying for.

You can save your progress and come back to your additional application at any time.
